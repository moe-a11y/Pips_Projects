# Privacy Policy — Pip's Projects

_Last updated: July 6, 2026_

## 1. Overview

Pip's Projects operates an internal publishing application (the "App") that posts our own original animated videos to our own official social media accounts (YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok). This policy explains what data the App handles.

## 2. Who uses the App

Only we do. The App has no public sign-up and is never used by, or on behalf of, any third party. It does not collect data from viewers of our videos.

## 3. Data the App handles

- **Our own account credentials**: OAuth tokens for our own official social media accounts (including our TikTok account, authorized via TikTok Login Kit). These are used exclusively to authenticate the App and publish our videos to our own accounts.
- **Basic account information**: when authorizing via TikTok Login Kit, the App may receive basic profile information (such as display name and avatar) of our own account, used only to confirm the correct account is connected.
- **Our own content**: the videos and captions we create and publish.

The App does **not** collect, store, or process any personal data of viewers, followers, or any other third parties. It does not use cookies, analytics, tracking, or advertising technologies.

## 4. Storage & security

Credentials are stored as encrypted secrets in our build infrastructure (GitHub Actions encrypted secrets) and are never published or shared. Access is limited to the account owner.

## 5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with anyone. Data flows only between the App and the social media platforms we publish to, under their respective terms.

## 6. Data retention & deletion

OAuth tokens are retained only while the App is in use and can be revoked at any time from the connected platform's settings (for TikTok: Settings → Security → Apps and websites). Revoking access immediately prevents the App from accessing the account.

## 7. Children

Our published videos are family-friendly, but the App itself collects no data from anyone, including children.

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
