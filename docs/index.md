# Pip's Projects

**Pip's Projects** is an original animated short-video series starring Pip the Otter, a curious little inventor who runs one whimsical experiment every day in his magical workshop — starring his Mystical Hydraulic Press.

New 8-second episodes are posted daily to our official channels on YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok.
